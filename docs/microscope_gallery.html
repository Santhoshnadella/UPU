Transistor-Level SEM Gate Analysis

An extreme nanometer-scale grayscale image captured natively using a Scanning Electron Microscope (SEM). At this mind-bending magnification, the logical components completely disappear, leaving only the physical topology of the silicon. The overlapping vertical and horizontal ridges are the lowest-level metal routing layers (M1/M2) packed with localized interconnected Vias, connecting Source and Drain junctions to build our custom NAND gates and Inverters underneath.
